Patient - Information about an individual receiving health-related services.
Coverage - Insurance card information.
Organization(Hospital) - Information about a hospital.
Organization(Insurance) - Information about an insurance company.
Practitioner - Personal information about a doctor and his qualifications.
PractitionerRole - Information about what specialty a practitioner works in an organization for a certain period of time.
ServiceRequest - General information about a request for transportation.
Task - Information about a transportation that can be performed and tracking the status of completion of this transportation.
